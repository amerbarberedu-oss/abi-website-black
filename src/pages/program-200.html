About the Program

Learn & Practice the Fundamentals

The Barbering Fundamentals course trains apprentice registrants to become competent registered apprentices, licensed cosmetologists to expand their services, and barber operators to hone their skills as Master Barbers.

Students attend a good-faith 200-hour course of practical and theoretical hands-on training in the practice of barbering under the supervision of a licensed instructor who assists each student in such practice. After a barber apprentice completes two years of apprenticeship in a licensed barbershop, they may qualify to sit for the barber exam for the Barber Operator's License.

Our curriculum also provides a complete overview of day-to-day haircut services, expectations, precautions and safeguards every barber apprentice will encounter when their apprenticeship starts in a barbershop.
