We Take the Guesswork Out of Licensure

It's easy to get confused and frustrated when dealing with state and local licensing laws. The time and attention needed to obtain a license can be overwhelming. Applicants often have to prepare for the N.Y. State Exam or attend Appearance Enhancement School while maintaining their work schedule and juggling family obligations. Who has time left to prepare a lengthy application requiring an exorbitant amount of documentation?

No Need To Worry — ABI Handles It All

Our staff manages the entire licensing process from start to finish. We oversee our clients' applications and work hard until they're approved. ABI has the knowledge and resources to handle special circumstances that would otherwise stall or delay the licensing process — and the expertise to get your application properly submitted to the Division of Licensing Services.
