About the Program

An Exciting Field, Mastered In Four Weekends

Scalp micro-pigmentation is a cosmetic tattooing procedure that applies permanent pigments to the skin to enhance features or camouflage imperfections. This comprehensive program serves both beginners and experienced practitioners in cosmetic tattooing.

Our experienced instructors guide you through a combination of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practice, giving you the confidence to work on real clients. You'll learn to create a natural-looking hairline that complements each client's facial features, and to replicate the look of stubble or a full head of hair for clients who want more density.

By the end of the program, you'll be able to provide clients a safe, effective, long-lasting solution for balding or thinning hair — with the knowledge and skills to start your own SMP business or expand your existing services.

You Will Learn

  • Client Consultation & Assessment
  • Hairline Design & Mapping
  • Pigment Color Selection
  • Micro-Pigmentation Device Technique
  • Sterilization & Hygiene
  • Client Expectations & Aftercare

Who Can Enroll

Holders of a NYS Cosmetology, Barber, or Esthetics license — plus permanent makeup artists, medical spa employees, plastic surgeons, beauty consultants, medical professionals, makeup artists, tattoo artists, and microblading practitioners.
