Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your personal information.

Our Commitment To You

At the American Barber Institute, we prioritize the protection and security of your personal information. We highly value our relationship with you and work diligently to maintain your privacy while honoring your preferences. As part of our relationship, there may be instances where we need to collect or share certain information about you. This privacy policy statement outlines how we handle customer information.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide to us through applications, surveys, registration forms, and other similar channels.

How We Share Information

We do not disclose any information we possess about you to any third party unless required by law or in response to legal requests.

In certain circumstances, the law permits us to share information about our current and former customers with government agencies or authorized third parties. This disclosure may be necessary in response to subpoenas or to comply with specific legal obligations.

How We Protect Information

We prioritize the security of your data and take precautions to safeguard it from unauthorized access.

Former Customers

We do not share any information regarding our former customers with any external party unless compelled to do so by law or legal obligations.

Please note that this policy is subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to you promptly. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our policy or privacy practices, please contact us.
