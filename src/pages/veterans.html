About Our Veterans Program

From Service To The Chair

At A.B.I., the guiding principle of barbering education is to provide our students the education to work in a sustainable, nearly recession-proof occupation — and to instill in our graduates a sense of pride in their work. It's no surprise that a special set of veterans desire to become barbers: one of the most challenging, yet fun and relaxing jobs around.

If you're a veteran interested in stepping into the world of barbering, you've come to the right place. We know finding employment and training as a veteran can be a challenge — that's why we're here.

With programs approved by the New York State Division of Veterans' Affairs (VA), the American Barber Institute can equip you with all the skills required to become a Master Barber. Our programs are approved for the training of veterans and other eligible persons in accordance with the provisions of Title 38 United States Code § 3676.

What You'll Need
