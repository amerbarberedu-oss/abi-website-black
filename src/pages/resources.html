Help With Cost, Benefits & Licensing

ACCES-VR Financial Assistance

A New York State program for individuals with documented disabilities. If you qualify, it can cover tuition, tools and supplies — at no cost to you. ABI assists with the referral process.

Learn about ACCES-VR →

Veterans & GI Bill®

ABI accepts the Post-9/11 GI Bill® (Ch. 33), VR&E (Ch. 31), Montgomery GI Bill® and DEA (Ch. 35). We certify your enrollment with the VA so benefits can cover all or part of tuition.

VA education benefits →

Licensing — NY State Board

Every student is prepared for the New York State Board of Barbering Examination (written + practical). Pass it and you receive your Master Barber License, required to work in any NY barbershop.

500-Hour program →

Tools & Supplies

Students purchase their own tools, books and supplies — directly from ABI at registration or from approved third-party suppliers. A detailed tools list is provided upon registration.
