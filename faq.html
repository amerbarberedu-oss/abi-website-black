Frequently Asked Questions

Everything students ask us most — costs, schedules, requirements and more. Don't see your question? Call (212) 290-2289 and we'll answer it on the spot.

Why choose A.B.I.?

A.B.I. is located in the heart of NYC, minutes from Times Square, Penn Station and Grand Central Station. Our Master Barber program enables any successful graduate to meet the requirements to take the New York State Board Exam for the Master Barber license — which opens the doors to abundant employment opportunities and qualifies you to own a barbershop and employ Master Barbers or apprentices. ABI has had a graduation rate of over 80% in the last three consecutive years, and provides placement services to all of our graduates.

How much will the programs cost at A.B.I.?

The 500-Hour Master Barber Program is $5,600 for the morning schedule and $4,600 for the afternoon or weekend schedules, without supplies. The 200-Hour Fundamentals Program is $3,600. The 50-Hour Refresher Program is $1,500 without supplies. The Scalp Micro-Pigmentation Program is $3,500 including a tool kit. The Contagious Diseases home-study course is $100. Every program has a payment plan so you can pay while you attend.

What will we learn at A.B.I.?

In our 500-hour program, future barbers receive four months (about seventeen weeks) of instruction, including two weeks of barber theory: sanitation, sterilization, barber history, barber laws and shop management. You'll work on real clients and learn the art of shaving and hairstyling — classic tapers, faux hawks, pompadours, bald heads, caesars, low/mid/high fades, high-top fades, afros, flat tops, razor lineups, shampoos, classical haircuts, beard trims, shape ups, blowouts, mohawks and more — plus facial massage and hot towel shaving, in preparation for the New York State Board Exam.

How long will the 500-Hour Barber Program take?

Approximately four months. Because some students have tough schedules, we offer morning, afternoon, and part-time weekend classes — the Master Barber Program includes 30 hours of instruction per week for 17 weeks.

How does A.B.I. help with jobs?

A.B.I. offers placement services to our graduates through a full-time job placement office. Our completion rates are above 80%, and our priority is facilitating connections between graduates and prospective barbershops, spas, and salons looking to employ.

What sets ABI apart from other schools?

At ABI, everyone has a collective responsibility for teaching and learning. Our students learn primarily by cutting clients' hair, because our staff has a deep-rooted passion and understanding of what it takes to make it in the real world. All of our current teachers are former ABI students.

I have my New York Cosmetology license but would like to advance my barbering skills.

A.B.I. offers a 50-Hour Barber Refresher program intended for licensed individuals — Cosmetologists, Hairdressers, and Barber Apprentices — wishing to enhance their skills. We make sure every student is up to speed on classical haircutting, straight razor shaves, fades, tapers, scissor-over-comb techniques and modern hairstyles, and prepared to pass the New York State Barbering Licensing Examination.

How old do I have to be to enroll?

You must be 17 years of age to attend. Students under 18 need the permission of their parents to enroll.

I have a disability. Will I still be able to attend?

Yes. If you are eligible for training and meet the qualifications for funding, ACCESS-VR will bear the expenses of the program and pay the American Barber Institute directly — tuition, tools, and books are all covered. See our ACCESS-VR page for details. Our facility is also handicap accessible.

Do we still have to come to school during holidays?

No — we are closed for all legal holidays. See the holiday calendar.

How long have you been in business?

American Barber Institute was established in 1996 — we've been in business for over 30 years, serving the community.

When do classes start?

New classes begin on the first Monday of each month. Check our schedule for the next starting date, and call us at (212) 290-2289 to get started.
