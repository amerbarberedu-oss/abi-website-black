Job Placement

A.B.I. maintains a full-time job placement office for graduates — and for students who wish to work part-time while still attending school. Many of our students graduate with multiple job offers waiting.

How Placement Works

We Connect You With Shops That Are Hiring

Our Placement Office consistently reaches out to barbershop and salon owners to inform them of our available labor pool. We collect data on their shop needs and preferences, and make a concerted effort to match graduates with businesses where both employer and employee benefit.

We cannot guarantee placement for any particular student — many factors go into obtaining and keeping a job as a professional barber. Beyond practical skills, personal and professional conduct are imperative in this industry. During training, we expose students to the realistic needs of an employer: how to present yourself in an interview, and the boundaries of proper conduct between you, your employer, and clientele.
