Basic Requirements

Entrance Requirements

  • Social Security Card or Tax ID Number
  • High School Diploma (HSD) or GEDDon't have either? ABI administers an Ability-To-Benefit (ATB) entrance exam prior to enrolling. Foreign diploma? Submit a notarized translation and a copy of the original document.
  • Must be at least 17 years of ageStudents under 18 need parental permission to enroll.
  • Proof of residential address
  • Valid photo identification or driver's license
  • Down payment$500 for the 500-hour program; varies by program.
