About American Barber Institute

Where NYC Learns The Craft

Founded in 1996, A.B.I. is in its fourth decade of turning ambition into licensed careers. Our two-floor, 3,000 sq ft school in the heart of Manhattan is one of the largest of its kind in New York City — minutes from Penn Station, Grand Central and Times Square.

Students immerse themselves in barber theory — sanitation, sterilization, barber history, law and shop management — then refine their craft on a steady stream of real clients under licensed instructors. Every single instructor is an ABI graduate.

  • Licensed by NYS Department of EducationCurriculum, programs and instructors all state-approved.
  • Real clients, real experienceHundreds of haircuts before you graduate — not mannequins.
  • NYS Board Exam preparationGraduate fully equipped to earn your Master Barber license.
  • Job placement officeMany graduates finish with multiple offers waiting.

How to Get Started

Enrolling Is Simple

Our Admissions Office will walk you through everything — and if your circumstances are extenuating, talk to us. We work with you to remove any obstacle between you and a better life.

Call or Visit

Call (212) 290-2289 or book a free tour of the school — English and Spanish spoken.

Bring Your Documents

Social Security card or Tax ID, photo ID, proof of address, and your HSD/GED — no diploma? Take our entrance exam instead.

Pick Your Schedule

Morning, afternoon, or weekend sessions — 17 weeks to a new career, with payment plans while you study.

Start Cutting

New classes begin the first Monday of every month. Your chair is waiting.
